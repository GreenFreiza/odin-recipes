Odin Recipes

One for the Taste Enthusiasts

In this first endeavour I bring you, Odin Recipes. This is a three-course meal themed after the richest and most fun country in Africa, South Africa.(Opinion may be biased)

Being such a diverse nation, there were many tasty and unique dishes to choose from, so I kept it very neutral with this three-course meal option:

  • Delicious Bacon Rings as a starter,

  • The main dish being a savoury Cape Malay Chicken Curry dish, and

  • a sugary sweet Malva Puding for dessert.

These are my picks for the theme South Africa.

Try them and enjoy!!
