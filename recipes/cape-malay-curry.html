Savoury Cape Malay Chicken Curry

Description

This one pot of Cape Malay Chicken Curry will tickle your tastebuds with a unique mix of spices. It’s traditionally eaten without utensils (try using your hands – it’s fun!). This is a Proudly South African meal, hailing from the Southern-most tip of the country.

Being so savoury and spicy, it would be a crime not to try out this meal. All you need is:

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (mix of thighs & drumsticks, skin on)
  • 250 g white onion (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 60 g fresh ginger (grated)
  • 3 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 2 tsp. ground coriander
  • 1 tsp. ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp. chilli powder
  • 4 cardamom pods (lightly crushed)
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 sticks of cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 1 tsp salt (more to taste)
  • 1.5 cups chicken stock
  • 200 g tinned tomatoes (crushed)
  • 1 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 2 potatoes (peeled and cut into chunks)
