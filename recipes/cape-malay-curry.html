Savoury Cape Malay Chicken Curry

Description

This one pot of Cape Malay Chicken Curry will tickle your tastebuds with a unique mix of spices. It’s traditionally eaten without utensils (try using your hands – it’s fun!). This is a Proudly South African meal, hailing from the Southern-most tip of the country.
