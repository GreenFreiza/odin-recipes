Delicious Bacon Rings

Description

If you fancy making yourself a little treat, or starter to an amazing meal, try these tasty bacon wrapped onion rings. A twist on the traditional onion rings where we replace the breadcrumbs with juicy streaky bacon. A perfect snack to get those taste-buds going.

It may not be a South African invention, but it's still staple of the nation nonetheless, and here's how you make it:

Ingredients

  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 12 strips bacon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
  • Sauce of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. Line a medium baking sheet with aluminum foil and fit with a metal cooling rack.
  2. Cut onion into 1/2" rings, then separate every other ring, so that each ring contains two rounds of onion.
  3. Stir together brown sugar and cayenne pepper on a large shallow plate, then lay bacon on mixture to coat.
  4. Wrap doubled up rings with bacon, then set on baking sheet.
  5. Bake 30 to 35 minutes, or until bacon is crisp and golden.
  6. Serve with sauce of your choice. (Yields 6 servings)
